    Economy 2: Sustainable and well-maintained infrastructure and services for all, through inclusive and effective partnerships

    Policy objectives

    Indicators

    ECO2.1 Increase access to safe, reliable and affordable modern energy services for all that are increasingly generated from renewable sources and reduce reliance on imported fossil fuels
    ECO2.1.1 Reported online Proportion of households sourcing energy for lighting from renewable sources
    ECO2.1.2 Exploring data sources Total number of mini-grid renewable energy systems in rural communities by province
    ECO2.2 Ensure all people have reliable access to safe drinking water and sanitation infrastructure
    ECO2.2.1 Reported online Proportion of population with reliable access to safe drinking water
    ECO2.2.2 Reported online Proportion of population with reliable access to improved sanitation facilities
    ECO2.3 Ensure that all public infrastructure, including health, education and sports facilities are safe, accessible, secure and maintained in compliance with building codes and standards
    ECO2.3.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of new buildings in compliance with the building code
    ECO2.3.2 Exploring data sources Proportion of existing buildings repaired in compliance with the building code
    ECO2.4 Enact clear infrastructure governance, legislative frameworks and standards for resilient infrastructure and maintenance
    ECO2.4.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of established road and building standards implemented
    ECO2.5 Improve partnerships and cost effective use of resources to ensure sustainable asset management and maintenance
    ECO2.5.1 Exploring data sources Variance from approved asset management budget
    ECO2.6 Provide equitable and affordable access to efficient transport in rural and urban areas
    ECO2.6.1 Reported online Proportion of population with access to transport by road, sea and air
    ECO2.7 Ensure compliance with international conventions and standards for safe and secure transport
    ECO2.7.0 Exploring data sources ECO 2.7.1 Total number of external audits undertaken to establish compliance with conventions and standards and expose non-conformity
    ECO2.8 Establish effective partnerships that facilitate the development of the private sector and rural communities as service suppliers in the provision of transport and the infrastructure sector
    ECO2.8.1 Exploring data sources Proportion of procurement contracts issued for Island Based Contractors, Community Based Contractors
    ECO2.9 Increase use of and access to information and communications technologies, including on-line government services
    ECO2.9.1 Reported online % of population having access to broadband connection
    ECO2.9.2 Exploring data sources Annual change in total number of users of on-line government services
